About Me

DMAN - Metalurgie, Iasi, Romania

  • The '90s

    Lost nights with Mihai (from song "1, 2 si 3 de 4") watching YoMtvRaps

    Wu-Tang Clan, Dr Dre, The Roots, DaLuniz, 2Pac, NaughtyByNature, SnoopDogg, Biggy plants "the seed".

  • '98

    "Clung" on to Dahey from Rool LK band and starts Vineri 13 project.

  • 1998 - 2001

    Makes 2 albums as Vineri 13 with Dahey and Johnny and bonds lifetime friendship.

  • 2001 - 2005

    New collaboration projects:

    • DOMINO (with Johnny and Seba)
    • PADRE (with Andrei)
    • RBK (with Angela)
    • KK (with PDV, Vineri 13 and Mihai)

  • 2008 - 2009

    The Rock "seed", which was previously planted by Paul Birzu's guitar plays and collaborations with GOD band on Vineri 13 albums, finally blooms on DMan's "land of Hip-Hop" with MORPHINE project (with Dahey).

  • 2011 - 2015

    New collaboration with the rock band Rosu si Negru which was re-established in Iasi by the tenor Cosmin Marcovici. This collaboration has led to 2 new songs (Inger si Demon and O poveste), rehearsals and concerts in Iasi. The band was later on renamed to Karma.

  • 2018

    New single: Las' ca-i bine

  • 2019

    New single: Omul din oglinda (Sky above) with Erik (online artist)

  • 2020

    New single: Nu ne pasa with Canaf and Zeni (Razvan Boroda) recorded at Dahey's Wicked Studio

  • 2020

    After 5 years, Nimic a spune single is finally finished with the help of Dahey and the tenor Dragos Ionel at the Wicked Studio.

  • 2021

    Collaborating with Johnny, Dahey and Seba for a new single Digital Me
